# Customer Churn Prediction App

This is a professional Streamlit app that predicts customer churn for businesses.
It can handle large datasets (30,000+ rows) and automatically clean data, handle missing values,
and provide individual predictions, summary statistics, and actionable recommendations
to retain high-risk customers.

## Features
- Upload any CSV dataset (Churn column optional)
- Predict individual customer churn probability
- Summary of total predicted churn / stay
- High-risk customer analysis with suggested actions
- Handles missing and irregular data automatically

## Tools & Technologies
- Python 3
- Streamlit
- Pandas, Numpy
- Scikit-learn (Logistic Regression)
- Joblib (model saving/loading)

## How to Use
1. Upload your CSV dataset
2. Click on "Predict Churn"
3. View individual predictions, summary statistics, and suggested actions
